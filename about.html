About Ahmed Bakr

Ahmed Bakr is a passionate programming engineer with a strong academic background and years of professional experience in the field of software development. He graduated from the Faculty of Engineering, where he majored in Computer Science, a discipline that deepened his love for technology and problem-solving. His education provided him with the skills to understand complex programming languages, algorithms, and system designs.

Over the years, Ahmed realized that his true passion lay not just in coding, but in sharing his knowledge with others. His teaching journey began with volunteering at local workshops and later expanded to offering structured programming courses for both children and adults. His approach focuses on making coding accessible and fun for all ages, turning what might seem like a difficult subject into an exciting and interactive experience.
