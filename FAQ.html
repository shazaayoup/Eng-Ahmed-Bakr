Frequently Asked Questions

What age group are the courses suitable for?

The courses are suitable for both kids and adults. We offer different tracks based on age and experience level.

How long is each course?

Each course lasts for 3 months, with 1 lessons per week. You will have access to all course materials during this period.

Do I need prior programming experience?

No, our courses are designed for beginners. We start with the basics and gradually build up to more complex concepts.
